Features

Geo-fenced Campaigns. Worldwide. From the comfort of your couch.

Deploy targeted campaigns across the globe with the click of a button. Ads on wheels provides you the capability to put your content infront of the most relevant audience by simply drawing a polygon on a map.

Advanced analytics. Clear directives.

We use a mixture of data provided to us by the local government and surveys by our team to come up with the most accurate impact numbers. We then crunch those numbers to come up with actionable KPIs that can help your marketing efforts

Datapoints. Visualized.

Heatmaps are a great way to visualize where your content was displayed. Ads on Wheels provides an up to the second, accurate visualization of your data so that you are always in the know.

Campaign Reports. Automated.

Ads on wheels generates high quality, content rich PDF reports at the completion of your campaign. These reports provide clear business intelligence that you can add as an attachment or quickly reference. We do this, so you don't have to.

Billing. Secure. Managed.

Clean and transparent billing gives you the peace of mind that your money is safe with us. You can add to and withdraw from your account whenever you want. And of course, we use state-of-the-art encryption to make sure your data is always secure.

The Value of Ads on Wheels

DOOH is the future

DOOH is one of the fastest growing and most engaging forms of advertisment. Programmatic access to digital screens allows the advertiser unprecedented control over their ad campaign. Studies have shown that it also has one of the highest recall rates of any advertisment.

Ads on Wheels takes your content to your audience

Geo-fenced and dynamic ads allow you to place your content in front of the most relevant demographic for your ad. This means that more peoplpe that are likely to buy your product see your ad. Your ad only gets played in an area that you define and at a time that you decide.

Real-time analytics provides key insights

Access to real-time data about your campaign allows you to have a clear understanding of how to optimize your marketing strategy. A state-of-the-art dashboard allows you to draw correlation between the successs of the campaign and your sales data.

Massive savings in cost

Ads on wheels allows you to lower your DOOH marketing costs substantially. We are able to provide the same amount of ad space at a fraction of the cost of static billboards. And since we are digital, we are able to cut out the middle-men and we pass these saving on to our customers.

Ease of deployment

You can depoly a global ad campaign in under a minute. There is no need for large distributed workforce to manage your ad strategy. Also, a pretty UI never hurt anyone.
